Roots — why we exist

Every diagnosis rewrites a family's story. We help write in some softness.

A degenerative disease doesn't just change one life — it reshapes routines, finances, and the quiet moments that used to feel ordinary. Legacy of Love exists to meet families in that shift with something tangible: a box of comfort, practicality, and care, put together by people who see them.

Dignity first

Every package is chosen to support independence and comfort — never pity. We ask what would actually help, then we build around that answer.

Made for the whole family

Degenerative illness touches caregivers as much as patients. Our packages include something for the people holding everything together, too.

No wrong time to reach out

Whether it's a new diagnosis or year twelve of caregiving, there's no "too early" or "too late" to ask for a little support.

Trunk — how it works

A simple path from request to doorstep.

1

Tell us your story

A patient, caregiver, or loved one fills out a short request form — the diagnosis, the daily reality, what would genuinely help.

2

We curate with care

Our volunteers hand-select items suited to that specific journey, from comfort pieces to practical tools to small joys.

3

It arrives, with a note

The package ships directly to their door with a handwritten note — a reminder that someone outside their four walls is thinking of them.

Leaves — what's inside

Six small things, chosen on purpose.

Every Legacy of Love package is simple by design — nothing overwhelming, just a handful of things meant to bring warmth, comfort, and a moment of ease.

A handwritten note

A personal message, written by one of our volunteers — a reminder that someone outside their four walls is thinking of them.

A sticker sheet

A little sheet of joy — for decorating a journal, a walker, a window, or wherever feels right.

Hot chocolate, x2

Two packets — one for them, one to share with whoever's sitting with them that day.

A fidget toy

Something for restless hands — grounding on the hard days, distracting on the long ones.

An adult coloring book & supplies

A calm, low-pressure way to pass time — pencils or markers included, ready to use right out of the box.

A blanket

Soft, warm, and easy to keep close — on the couch, in bed, or wherever they spend most of their day.
